About Semptify

Built to protect the rights of people facing housing problems. Free forever. No ads. Privacy-first.

Our Mission

Housing is a human right. Yet millions of people face eviction, unsafe conditions, rent hikes, and retaliation every year — often without the resources to defend themselves. Semptify exists to level the playing field.

We build tools that help tenants, advocates, and legal professionals organize evidence, track deadlines, generate documents, and communicate securely. Everything we make is designed for people under stress, with limited time, limited money, and a lot at stake.

What We Believe

🛡️

Privacy First

Your documents stay in your cloud storage. We do not sell data, run ads, or train AI on your files. You control everything.

⚖️

Truth From Both Sides

We do not assume any party is automatically right. We build for facts, records, chronology, and evidence.

🆓

Free Forever

We will never charge tenants for core access. No paywalls, no subscriptions, no freemium traps.

📋

Evidence Over Novelty

Every feature is judged by whether it improves rights protection, evidence integrity, or user control.

🔒

Transparent & Auditable

Open-source by default. Deterministic workflows. No hidden state that weakens accountability.

🤝

Built With, Not For

We design with housing advocates, legal aid clinics, and tenants — not in isolation.

Who We Serve

  • Tenants — Organize documents, track deadlines, communicate with landlords, and preserve evidence
  • Advocates — Manage multiple clients, review evidence, send documents, and coordinate cases
  • Legal Professionals — Access organized case files, track signatures, and manage court deadlines
  • Researchers & Policy Makers — Access anonymized insights to improve housing policy

How It Works

Semptify connects to your personal cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive). Your documents live there — not on our servers. We provide the organization layer: timeline tracking, document tagging, secure delivery, and evidence generation. When you leave, your documents stay with you.

Open Source & Community

Semptify is open-source software. Anyone can inspect the code, suggest improvements, or run their own instance. We believe housing justice tools should be public infrastructure, not proprietary products.

We welcome contributors — developers, designers, housing advocates, legal professionals, and tenants. If you want to help, reach out.

Semptify is a labor of conviction. This project was built by people who believe that technology should serve those who need it most — not those who can pay the most. We are sustained by grants, donations, and volunteer effort. We do not have investors, we do not have a "growth team," and we do not optimize for engagement.
