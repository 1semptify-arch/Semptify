Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Core Principle Your data stays in your cloud storage. Semptify does not own your documents, does not sell your data, and does not use your data to train AI models. We are a privacy-first platform built for people who need to protect their rights.

1. What Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: name, email address, and role selection (tenant, advocate, legal, manager)
  • Documents you upload to your personal vault
  • Timeline events, messages, and case notes you create
  • Generated letters and checklist responses

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • OAuth authentication tokens (stored encrypted, used only for cloud storage access)
  • Basic system logs (errors, uptime) for platform reliability
  • No tracking pixels, no advertising cookies, no analytics scripts

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  1. Provide the Semptify service (vault storage, document delivery, timeline, tools)
  2. Authenticate you and secure your account
  3. Maintain and improve platform stability
  4. Comply with legal requirements (subpoena, court order) if applicable

3. Where Your Data Lives

User-Controlled Cloud Storage Your documents are stored in your own Google Drive or OneDrive, in a Semptify folder you control. You can revoke access, delete the folder, or download everything at any time. Semptify does not maintain copies of your documents on our servers except temporary cache during upload.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers
  • We do not use your documents to train AI models
  • We do not track you across the web
  • We do not share your data with landlords, property managers, or opposing parties
  • We do not run behavioral analytics or profiling

5. Blockchain Timestamping

When you upload documents to your vault, you may optionally generate a blockchain timestamp. This creates a cryptographic proof of existence at a point in time. The timestamp does not contain the document content — only a hash (fingerprint). This is for your evidence preservation. We cannot reverse a hash to recover your document.

6. Document Delivery

When you send a document to another Semptify user (Review Required, Signature Required, or Message), the recipient receives a notification and can access the document in their vault. The sender identity, role, and timestamp are recorded. Recipients must explicitly accept or sign documents — they never become tenant-owned automatically. You control who can send you documents through your role permissions.

7. Data Retention

  • Your documents: retained in your cloud storage for as long as you keep them there
  • Account information: retained while your account is active; deleted upon request
  • System logs: retained for 90 days for debugging, then purged

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download all your data
  • Delete your account and associated metadata
  • Revoke cloud storage access at any time
  • Request corrections to your account information
  • Export your timeline and vault index

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: HTTPS everywhere, encrypted OAuth tokens, access controls by role, and rate limiting. However, no system is perfectly secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your cloud storage account and device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes by email and by posting an update on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We will never reduce your privacy rights without explicit consent.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@semptify.org.

This privacy policy is a living document. Semptify is built on the principle that your data belongs to you. If we ever need to change that, we will tell you plainly and give you a choice.
