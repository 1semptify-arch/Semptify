📋 Your Deadlines

📜 Key Minnesota Legal Deadlines (Reference)

Situation Deadline Notes
Respond to eviction summons (Answer)
Minn. Stat. § 504B.321		 7 days from service File written answer with the court. Missing this = default judgment.
Security deposit return by landlord
Minn. Stat. § 504B.178		 21 days after move-out Landlord must return deposit + itemized statement. Failure allows you to sue for 2x the amount.
Notice to quit before eviction (non-payment)
Minn. Stat. § 504B.135		 14 days written notice Landlord must give 14 days written notice to pay or quit before filing eviction.
Landlord notice to enter (non-emergency)
Minn. Stat. § 504B.211		 24 hours advance notice Landlord must give reasonable notice; 24 hours is the standard. Emergencies are exempt.
Landlord provide written lease
Minn. Stat. § 504B.111		 Before or at move-in Required for leases of 12+ months. You have a right to a written copy.
Landlord disclose name/address for notices
Minn. Stat. § 504B.181		 At or before move-in Landlord must disclose who to send notices to.
Tenant file rent escrow action
Minn. Stat. § 504B.385		 After reasonable time for repairs After written repair request goes unaddressed, tenant may deposit rent with court.
Respond to lease non-renewal
Minn. Stat. § 504B.145		 Check lease terms Your lease may require notice 30–60 days before end. Check your specific lease.
